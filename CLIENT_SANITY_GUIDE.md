## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create a Sanity Account

1. **Go to Sanity.io**
   - Visit [sanity.io](https://www.sanity.io)
   - Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Get Started"** in the top right

2. **Sign Up Options**
   - You can sign up with:
     - **Google** (recommended - fastest)
     - **GitHub**
     - **Email** (create a password)

3. **Complete Registration**
   - Follow the prompts to create your account
   - Verify your email if required

### Step 2: Get Access to Your Studio

**Option A: If you received an invitation email**
- Check your email for an invitation from Sanity
- Click the **"Accept Invitation"** link
- You'll be automatically added to the project

**Option B: If you need to be added manually**
- Contact your developer to add you to the project
- They'll need your email address to send an invitation

### Step 3: Access Your Studio

Once you have access, you can access the Studio in two ways:

**Option 1: Deployed Studio (Recommended)**
- Your developer will provide you with a URL like:
  - `https://vendetta-roasting.sanity.studio`
- Bookmark this URL for easy access
- This works from any device with internet

**Option 2: Local Studio (Advanced)**
- Only if your developer set this up for you
- Usually accessed at: `http://localhost:3333`

- **Draft** - Changes saved but not visible on your website yet

**Important:** Always click **"Publish"** (not just "Save") to make changes visible on your website!

## Step-by-Step Editing Guides

### How to Edit Homepage Content

1. **Open Homepage Content**
   - Click **"Homepage Content"** in the left sidebar
   - You should see one document (there's only one homepage)

2. **Edit Hero Section**
   - **Site Title** - Main title shown at the top
   - **Hero Subtitle** - Text below the title
   - **Hero Background Image** - Click to upload or change
   - **Primary Button Text** - Usually "Shop Coffee"
   - **Secondary Button Text** - Usually "Subscribe"

3. **Edit Featured Products**
   - Scroll to **"Featured Products"** section
   - **Section Title** - Title for the featured section
   - **Section Description** - Optional description text
   - **Featured Products** - Click **"Add item"** to select products
   - You can add up to 6 products
   - Click on a product to select it from your product list

4. **Edit About Section**
   - **About Section Title** - Usually "Our Story"
   - **About Section Content** - Add or edit paragraphs
   - **About Button Text** - Usually "Learn More"

5. **Edit Subscription Section**
   - **Subscription Title** - Title for subscription section
   - **Subscription Description** - Description text
   - **Subscription Button Text** - CTA button text

6. **Save and Publish**
   - Click **"Publish"** button in the top right
   - Wait for confirmation
   - Changes appear on your website within 1-2 minutes

### How to Add or Edit a Product

1. **Open Products**
   - Click **"Products"** in the left sidebar

2. **Add New Product**
   - Click **"Create"** button (top right)
   - Select **"Product"**

3. **Fill in Product Details**

   **Basic Information:**
   - **Product Name** - e.g., "Ethiopian Yirgacheffe"
   - **Slug** - Auto-generated from name (you can edit if needed)
   - **Description** - Full product description
   - **Short Description** - Brief description for product cards (max 500 characters)

   **Images:**
   - Click **"Add item"** under Product Images
   - Upload an image (JPG, PNG, WebP, or GIF)
   - Add **Alt Text** (description for accessibility)
   - Check **"Primary Image"** for the main product image
   - You can add multiple images

   **Pricing:**
   - **Price (USD)** - Regular customer price (required)
   - **Wholesale Price (USD)** - Optional wholesale price

   **Product Details:**
   - **Category** - Select from existing categories or create new
   - **SKU** - Product code (optional)
   - **Weight (grams)** - Package weight
   - **Origin** - Country/region where coffee is from
   - **Roast Level** - Light, Medium, Dark, etc.
   - **Flavor Notes** - e.g., "Chocolate, Caramel, Citrus"

   **Inventory:**
   - **Inventory Quantity** - Current stock level
   - **Low Stock Threshold** - Alert when inventory drops below this number

   **Status:**
   - **Active** - Check to make product visible on website
   - **Featured** - Check to mark as featured product

   **SEO (Optional):**
   - **Meta Title** - For search engines
   - **Meta Description** - For search engines

4. **Save and Publish**
   - Click **"Publish"** button
   - Product will appear on your website

## Common Tasks

### Updating Product Inventory

1. Open **Products**
2. Click on the product you want to update
3. Find **"Inventory Quantity"** field
4. Enter the new quantity
5. Click **"Publish"**

**Note:** The system automatically decreases inventory when orders are placed.

---

### Changing Product Prices

1. Open **Products**
2. Click on the product
3. Find **"Price (USD)"** field
4. Enter the new price
5. Click **"Publish"**

---

### Making a Product Featured on Homepage

1. Open **Products**
2. Click on the product
3. Check the **"Featured"** checkbox
4. Click **"Publish"**

**Then:**
1. Open **Homepage Content**
2. Scroll to **"Featured Products"** section
3. Click **"Add item"**
4. Select the featured product
5. Click **"Publish"**

---

### Uploading Product Images

1. Open the product
2. Scroll to **"Product Images"** section
3. Click **"Add item"**
4. Click the image area to upload
5. Select your image file (JPG, PNG, WebP, or GIF)
6. Add **Alt Text** (describe the image)
7. Check **"Primary Image"** if this is the main image
8. Click **"Publish"**

**Tips:**
- Use high-quality images (at least 800x800 pixels)
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 2MB recommended)
- Use descriptive alt text for accessibility

---

### Editing Footer Information

1. Open **Site Settings**
2. Scroll to **"Footer Address"** section
3. Update any fields (address, email, phone)
4. Scroll to **"Social Media"** section
5. Update social media links
6. Click **"Publish"**

## Tips & Best Practices

### ‚úÖ Do's

- **Always click "Publish"** - Not just "Save"
- **Wait 1-2 minutes** after publishing for changes to appear
- **Use descriptive text** - Help customers understand your products
- **Add alt text to images** - Important for accessibility and SEO
- **Check "Active" checkbox** - To make content visible
- **Set expiration dates** - For time-sensitive announcements
- **Use high-quality images** - Makes your products look professional

### ‚ùå Don'ts

- **Don't delete products** - Instead, uncheck "Active" to hide them
- **Don't forget to publish** - Drafts won't appear on your website
- **Don't use very large images** - They'll load slowly (keep under 2MB)
- **Don't leave required fields empty** - The system will show errors

### üí° Pro Tips

1. **Preview Before Publishing**
   - Make all your edits first
   - Review everything
   - Then click "Publish" once

2. **Use Short Descriptions**
   - Product cards show short descriptions
   - Keep them concise and compelling

3. **Organize with Categories**
   - Use categories to group similar products
   - Makes browsing easier for customers

4. **Set Low Stock Thresholds**
   - Get alerts when inventory is running low
   - Helps you reorder in time

5. **Update Regularly**
   - Keep product information current
   - Update prices and inventory regularly
   - Add new products as they become available

## Troubleshooting

### Changes Not Appearing on Website

**Problem:** You published changes but they don't show on your website

**Solutions:**
1. **Wait 1-2 minutes** - Changes take time to appear (CDN caching)
2. **Hard refresh your browser:**
   - Windows/Linux: `Ctrl + Shift + R`
   - Mac: `Cmd + Shift + R`
3. **Check if you clicked "Publish"** - Not just "Save"
4. **Check "Active" checkbox** - Content must be active to show
5. **Try a different browser** - Sometimes browser cache causes issues

---

### Can't Upload Images

**Problem:** Image upload fails or shows error

**Solutions:**
1. **Check file format** - Use JPG, PNG, WebP, or GIF
2. **Check file size** - Try a smaller file (under 2MB)
3. **Try a different image** - The file might be corrupted
4. **Check internet connection** - Uploads require stable connection
5. **Try again later** - Sometimes temporary server issues

---

### Can't Find a Product

**Problem:** Product doesn't appear in the list

**Solutions:**
1. **Use the search bar** - Top of the Studio
2. **Check filters** - Make sure no filters are applied
3. **Check if product is active** - Inactive products might be hidden
4. **Scroll through the list** - Products are paginated

---

### Can't Edit Something

**Problem:** Field is grayed out or you can't click it

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your permissions** - Contact your developer if you need more access
2. **Make sure you're logged in** - Check top right corner
3. **Try refreshing the page** - Sometimes the interface needs a refresh

---

### Forgot Password

**Problem:** Can't log into Sanity

**Solutions:**
1. **Go to sanity.io**
2. **Click "Sign In"**
3. **Click "Forgot Password"**
4. **Enter your email**
5. **Check your email for reset link**
