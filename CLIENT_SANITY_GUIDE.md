# How to Edit Content on Your Website

## Quick Start

1. **Go to Sanity Studio**: https://pyoyob4y.sanity.studio/
2. **Log in** with your Sanity account
3. **Create or edit content**
4. **Click "Publish"**
5. **Wait 1-2 minutes** for changes to appear on your website

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Access Sanity Studio

Visit: **https://pyoyob4y.sanity.studio/**

You'll see a dashboard with content types on the left sidebar:
- **Announcement** - Site-wide announcements
- **Event** - Coffee events and workshops  
- **FAQ** - Frequently asked questions

### Step 2: Create New Content

**Example: Creating an Announcement**

1. Click **"Announcement"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"Create new"** button (top right)
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Title**: Enter your announcement title
   - **Slug**: Auto-generated from title (you can edit it)
   - **Content**: Write your announcement text
   - **Active**: Make sure this is checked ✅
   - **Priority**: Choose Low, Medium, High, or Urgent
   - **Published At**: Today's date (auto-filled)
   - **Expires At**: (Optional) Set when announcement should stop showing
4. Click **"Publish"** button (top right)

### Step 3: Edit Existing Content

1. Click the content type (e.g., "Announcement")
2. Click on the item you want to edit
3. Make your changes
4. Click **"Publish"** to save

### Step 4: Delete Content

1. Open the content item
2. Click the three dots menu (⋯) in the top right
3. Select **"Delete"**
4. Confirm deletion

## How Content Appears on Your Website

1. **You create/edit content** in Sanity Studio
2. **You click "Publish"**
3. **Content is saved** to Sanity
4. **Your website fetches** the content via API
5. **Content appears** on your website (may take 1-2 minutes due to caching)

## Important Notes

### Publishing
- Content must be **published** (not saved as draft) to appear on your website
- For announcements, make sure **"Active"** is checked ✅
- For events, the date must be in the **future** to appear

### Timing
- Changes appear on your website within **1-2 minutes** after publishing
- This delay is normal due to caching
- If you don't see changes, try refreshing your browser (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)

### Content Types

**Announcement**
- Use for: Sales, promotions, important notices
- Appears: Site-wide (wherever announcements are displayed)
- Must be: Published AND Active

**Event**
- Use for: Coffee tastings, workshops, community events
- Appears: Events calendar/page
- Must be: Published AND date in the future

**FAQ**
- Use for: Frequently asked questions
- Appears: FAQ page
- Must be: Published AND Active

## Troubleshooting

### Content not appearing on website?

1. **Check if it's published**: Content must be published, not saved as draft
2. **Check "Active" field**: For announcements and FAQs, make sure "Active" is checked
3. **Wait 1-2 minutes**: Changes take time to appear due to caching
4. **Refresh browser**: Hard refresh with Ctrl+F5 (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
5. **Check date**: For events, make sure the date is in the future

### Can't access Sanity Studio?

- Make sure you're logged in with the correct Sanity account
- Contact your developer if you need access
