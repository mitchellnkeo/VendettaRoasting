## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for a Great Demo

1. **Go slow** - Don't rush. Let them absorb each step
2. **Ask questions** - "Does this make sense?" "Any questions so far?"
3. **Show, don't just tell** - Actually create content, don't just explain
4. **Show the website** - Always show where content appears
5. **Be enthusiastic** - Your energy sets the tone
6. **Handle mistakes gracefully** - If something doesn't work, stay calm and troubleshoot
