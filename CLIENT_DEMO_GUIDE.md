## üé¨ Demo Walkthrough

### Demo 1: Creating an Announcement

**What it does:** Announcements appear at the top of your website to share important messages with customers.

**Steps:**
1. Click **"Announcement"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"Create new"** button (top right)
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Title**: "Holiday Sale - 20% Off All Coffee!"
   - **Content**: "Get 20% off all coffee beans this holiday season. Use code HOLIDAY20 at checkout."
   - **Priority**: Select "High" (this makes it more prominent)
   - **Active**: Make sure this is checked ‚úÖ
   - **Published At**: Today's date (auto-filled)
   - **Expires At**: (Optional) Set a date when the announcement should stop showing
4. Click **"Publish"** button (top right)
5. **Done!** The announcement will appear on your website

**Pro Tip:** Use the "Expires At" field to automatically remove announcements after a certain date.

### Demo 2: Adding a Coffee Tasting Event

**What it does:** Events appear on your website so customers can see upcoming coffee tastings, workshops, and community events.

**Steps:**
1. Click **"Event"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"Create new"** button

5. **Done!** The event is now listed on your website

**Pro Tip:** Only future events show on the website. Past events are automatically hidden.

## üé® Tips & Best Practices

### Images
- **Use high-quality images** - They make your website look professional
- **Optimize file sizes** - Large images slow down your site
- **Use descriptive filenames** - "coffee-beans.jpg" is better than "IMG_1234.jpg"

### Content
- **Write clearly** - Use simple, clear language
- **Be consistent** - Keep your tone and style consistent
- **Update regularly** - Fresh content keeps customers engaged

### Publishing
- **Always click "Publish"** - Drafts won't appear on your website
- **Check "Active" boxes** - Inactive content won't show
- **Set expiration dates** - For time-sensitive content like announcements

## üîÑ Making Changes

### Editing Existing Content
1. Click on the content type in the sidebar
2. Find the item you want to edit
3. Click on it
4. Make your changes
5. Click **"Publish"** to save

### Deleting Content
1. Open the content item
2. Click the **"..."** menu (top right)
3. Select **"Delete"**
4. Confirm deletion

### Unpublishing (Hiding) Content
1. Open the content item
2. Click **"Unpublish"** button
3. The content is hidden but not deleted (you can republish later)

---

## üì± Viewing Your Changes

After publishing content:

1. **Wait 1-2 minutes** - Changes need to sync to your website
2. **Visit your website** - Go to your live website URL
3. **Refresh the page** - Press Cmd+R (Mac) or Ctrl+R (Windows)
4. **See your changes!** - Your new content should appear

**Note:** Some content types (like Homepage Content) may require a page refresh or cache clear to see changes.

## üÜò Need Help?

### Common Issues

**"I can't see my changes on the website"**
- Make sure you clicked "Publish" (not just "Save")
- Wait 1-2 minutes for changes to sync
- Clear your browser cache (Cmd+Shift+R or Ctrl+Shift+R)
- Check that "Active" is checked (for announcements, FAQs, etc.)

**"I can't upload images"**
- Make sure the image file isn't too large (under 10MB recommended)
- Try a different image format (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
- Check your internet connection

**"I made a mistake"**
- Don't worry! You can always edit and republish
- Sanity keeps a history of changes, so you can revert if needed

### Getting Support

If you need help:
1. Contact your developer
2. Check the Sanity documentation: https://www.sanity.io/docs
3. Look for the "?" icon in Sanity Studio for in-app help
