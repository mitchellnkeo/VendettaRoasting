# Product Management Guide for Non-Technical Users

## Overview

Products are managed through **Sanity Studio**, a user-friendly content management system. You don't need any technical knowledge to add, edit, or manage products!

## Accessing Sanity Studio

1. **Go to Sanity Studio:**
   - URL: `https://vendetta-roasting.sanity.studio` (or the URL provided by your developer)
   - Log in with your Sanity account credentials

2. **Find Products:**
   - Once logged in, you'll see a sidebar on the left
   - Click on **"Product"** to see all products
   - Click **"Create new"** to add a new product

## Creating a New Product

1. Click **"Create new"** â†’ **"Product"**

2. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Product Name** (required) - e.g., "Ethiopian Yirgacheffe"
   - **Slug** (required) - This auto-generates from the name, but you can edit it
   - **Description** (required) - Full product description
   - **Short Description** - Brief description for product cards (max 500 characters)

3. Add Product Images:
   - Click **"Add item"** in the Images section
   - Upload an image
   - Set **Alt Text** (describe the image for accessibility)
   - Check **"Primary Image"** for the main product image
   - You can add multiple images

4. Set Pricing:
   - **Price (USD)** (required) - Regular customer price
   - **Wholesale Price (USD)** - Optional, for wholesale customers

5. Product Details:
   - **SKU** - Stock Keeping Unit (unique identifier)
   - **Category** - Select from existing categories
   - **Weight (grams)** - Product weight
   - **Origin** - e.g., "Colombia", "Ethiopia"
   - **Roast Level** - Select from dropdown (Light, Medium, Medium-Dark, Dark)
   - **Flavor Notes** - e.g., "Chocolate, Caramel, Citrus"

6. Inventory:
   - **Inventory Quantity** - Current stock (default: 0)
   - **Low Stock Threshold** - Alert when stock falls below this (default: 10)

7. Status:
   - **Active** - Check this to show product on website (checked by default)
   - **Featured** - Check to feature on homepage

8. SEO (Optional):
   - **SEO Meta Title** - Leave empty to use product name
   - **SEO Meta Description** - Description for search engines

9. Click **"Publish"** to save your product!

## Editing a Product

1. In Sanity Studio, click on **"Product"** in the sidebar
2. Find the product you want to edit
3. Click on it to open the editor
4. Make your changes
5. Click **"Publish"** to save

## Tips for Non-Technical Users

### Images
- **Use high-quality images** - They'll be automatically optimized
- **Set a primary image** - This is the main image shown on product listings
- **Add alt text** - Describe what's in the image (important for accessibility)

### Descriptions
- **Short Description** - Keep it brief (under 500 characters)
- **Full Description** - You can write more here, use it for detailed product information

### Inventory
- **Update regularly** - Keep inventory quantities accurate
- **Low Stock Threshold** - Set this to get alerts when stock is running low

### Status
- **Active** - Only active products show on the website
- **Featured** - Featured products appear prominently on the homepage

### Categories
- If you need a new category, contact your developer to add it
- Categories help customers find products

## Common Tasks

### Making a Product Inactive
1. Open the product in Sanity Studio
2. Uncheck **"Active"**
3. Click **"Publish"**

### Featuring a Product on Homepage
1. Open the product in Sanity Studio
2. Check **"Featured"**
3. Click **"Publish"**

### Updating Inventory
1. Open the product in Sanity Studio
2. Update **"Inventory Quantity"**
3. Click **"Publish"**

### Changing Price
1. Open the product in Sanity Studio
2. Update **"Price (USD)"** or **"Wholesale Price (USD)"**
3. Click **"Publish"**

## Important Notes

- **Always click "Publish"** after making changes - otherwise they won't be saved!
- **Changes appear immediately** on the website after publishing
- **Images are automatically optimized** - no need to resize them manually
- **Products sync to the database** automatically for order processing
